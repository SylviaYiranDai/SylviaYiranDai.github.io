IPQ - Production Log1

Posted on 2021-06-04

\My idea(s) for topic/title:**

At the beginning, my supervisor asked us to write some keywords on a paper. My keywords include Costa Rica, COVID-19, investment banks, government policy, living standards, and UN supervision. These are all my areas of interest. However, as some of the topics, like the government policy and UN supervision, is not closely linked with Economics, so I did not consider them anymore.

I found I could combine Costa Rica with COVID-19 together. Also, I am very interested in Uganda located in southeast Africa. At first, I chose to do some research into Costa Rica and Uganda. I hoped to compare the influences of COVID-19 on the two countries. I have chosen this topic not only due to my interest but also because the pandemic of COVID-19 has caused a variety of problems in all industries which need to be solved. The title I have chosen is “Analysis of the influence of COVID-19 and provision of developing suggestions—— using Costa Rica and Uganda as examples”.

The living standard is still a good topic because it is connected with everyone. I thought developing a model for everyone to work out the country with the best living standards might be a good choice. This is my other choice because this model may help people to find the most suitable country for themselves which will contribute to individual living standards.

\My preliminary research and development of my project ideas:**

I first did some research into Costa Rica and Uganda, and I found that the topic was too general to analyse. A country has many aspects which can be analysed, including its development, its economic system, its living standards, and so on. As a result, I decided to choose a representative industry. These two countries do have their popular industries. For example, Costa Rica is famous for medical equipment, food processing, and textiles and clothing. Uganda which depends more on the primary sector is strong in planting coffees, fishing, and growing maize. It is hard for me to find a representative industry in both countries. Finally, I decided to choose the aviation industry, because it is one of the strategic industries which was impacted by COVID-19 due to the travel ban and many other limitations. Then, I read some previous essays, for example, “The impacts of COVID-19 on the global airline industry: An event study approach” by Sakkakom Maneenop and Suntichai Kotcharin and “How COVID-19 is impacting and reshaping the airline industry” by Laurie Garrow and Virginie Lurkin. These essays provide me with an overall view of the current situation of the aviation industry. For example, I plan to find some information about the situation from the essays, such as the number of passengers and the changes in stock prices. Also, I may compare the essays which use different methods to work out the development plan to find a suitable method for me to use. My conclusion about the current situation and future development may hide in all this information.

When looking at the indicators of living standards which is the other interest I mentioned above, I found it had more things to do with Statistics instead of Economics, because we need to work out how to give weight to indicators when the users say they take one indicator into account most. As a result, I gave up this topic.

\My summary of the comments and advice from my supervisor (and specialist consultant if applicable):**

My supervisor talked to me. She thought my topic was good because it was narrow and was planned carefully with clear details. However, she thought Costa Rica and Uganda might not be good choices. This is because Costa Rica and Uganda are not developed or famous countries. This results in little data available for me to fully analyse the current situation and provide suggestions. Also, as I could not decide which method to use after reading several essays even after comparing the methods. She recommended I combined SWOT analysis with PEST analysis, as SWOT is more about the internal situation and PEST is about the external situation. The combination will make the suggestions more comprehensive.

\Modifications I have made as a result of my discussion with my supervisor (and specialist consultant if applicable):**

I started to look at the aviation industry in Costa Rica and Uganda and did some research. I found that there was exactly a lack of data due to low popularity. This is may also because the education level is low in these countries which causes that the number of local scholars is low as well. I chose to change from Costa Rica and Uganda to only the USA. Because of the contribution made by the government, the aviation industry in the USA has been recovered slowly. This is a frontier of recovery in the whole world.

Meanwhile, I spent some time reading around 14 methods that are useful for business analysis, including the famous ones like SWOT analysis and PEST analysis, and not famous ones like Benchmarking and EFE Matrix. I finally decide to use the SPACE Matrix with the help of SWOT analysis, and PEST analysis. This combination will make the suggestions comprehensive and scientific.

As a result, my title changed a little. It is now “Analysis of the influence of COVID-19 and provision of developing suggestions using the USA as an example with SPACE Matrix, SWOT Analysis and PEST Analysis”.

After reading the essays about the aviation industry and reports about airlines in the USA, I worked on several points I may pay attention to in future writing. These are “What can we conclude from the changes in the number of passengers and the number of seats offered?”; “What can we conclude from the income statements from different companies?”; “What can we conclude from the cash flow statements from different companies?”; “What can we conclude from the balance sheets from different companies?”; and “What can we conclude from the changes in stock prices?”. However, I may change these points in the future.

\My proposed title, aims and objectives:**

Title: Analysis of the influence of COVID-19 and provision of developing suggestions using USA as an example with SPACE Matrix, SWOT Analysis, and PEST Analysis

Aims: Analyse the performance of the aviation industry in the USA using several famous airline companies as examples during COVID-19. Mainly use the data from the website and other studies. Explain the influence of regulation from government and international organisations. Provide suggestions for future development.

Objectives:

  1. Discuss the overall impact of COVID-19 on the whole aviation industry
  2. Discuss the impact of COVID-19 on the USA aviation industry, including using data of share price, NYSE Arca Airline Index, passenger capacity, and volume of freight traffic
  3. Discuss the impact of regulations from both the US government and international organisations like the UN
  4. Discuss actions that have been taken to work out the future development suggestions
