IPQ - Production Log6

Posted on 2021-08-31

\Planned format of my 10 minute presentation (eg audience, use of visual aids, slides, use of notes, etc).**

For my presentation, I plan to have a 10-minute talk, which has two topics, including my IPQ report introduction, reflection, and prospective influences. The audience will include my supervisor, some teachers and other students in my class.

At the end of the presentation, there will be around 10 minutes, during which time my supervisor and audience are permitted to pose questions about any aspect of my project. As a result, I have gone through my presentation and report several times to make sure I am clear about every detail.

As I mentioned above, there are three topics. Each topic includes an averagely of four slides. I will use the slides for the presentation, which contain the bullet points about the topics and explain them more detailedly. There are some pictures on the slides to prevent the slides from looking dull. Meanwhile, to avoid forgetting some essential points, I made a list containing all the points I need to mention. The list can help me carry out the presentation more logically as well.

\Planned content of my presentation.**

• Project process:

My presentation will follow this plan:

At the beginning of the introduction, I will explain why I chose the topic related to the aviation industry and COVID-19. After that, I will discuss my aims and objectives to make sure the audiences are clear about my report structure. I will then talk about the methodology because I chose the SPACE Matrix Analysis, which is not famous. I will explain why I have chosen this topic and how I employed it. Then I will talk about the results of my report.

The second topic, the reflection, contains several points. I will go through the process of developing the report and then discuss both my success and failure. There are several skills or pieces of knowledge I have learnt as well. Finally, I will talk about my improvements in the future.

The last topic, which is the prospective influences, is mostly positive.

• Project findings and conclusions:

I pay attention to the influence of COVID-19 on United Airlines and use the SPACE Matrix Analysis to work out the future strategy for United Airlines. I looked into the data from several reports, including the cash flow statement, balance sheet, income statement and stock conditions. Also, I gave points to all the indicators of the SPACE Matrix to make sure the result was as correct as possible. In conclusion, United Airlines is suffering from COVID-19 and is in a pessimistic situation. I suggest the company take defensive actions, including decreasing the costs and investment.

\Modifications I have made as a result of rehearsal and/or discussion with my supervisor (and specialist consultant if applicable):**

I received primarily optimistic feedbacks on my presentation and slides. My supervisor thought the structure was clear, and the whole presentation was logical.

She provided me with several suggestions as well.

Firstly, in the introduction topic, she said the literature review part was too long to cover in 10 minutes. I made some modifications to this arrangement. I decided to use a slide to cover the literature review, which summarises all the essays. Also, I will move it to the topic of reflection when I talk about the things I have done during this process. This is because this change can make the introduction topic concentrate more on the results than the process. Meanwhile, the reflection can pay more attention to the process of developing reports.

Secondly, the design of my slides was not excellent. Although I chose only to use the bullet points to present, there were still too many words on the slide. As a result, I replaced some simple explanations with the pictures like the aeroplanes of United Airlines to remind the audience of my topic. However, this change would make my talking more difficult because it required me to remember more details in my mind. I decided to write a more completed note for me to prepare for the presentation.

Additionally, I still need to spend more time practising my presentation skills. At present, I rely on the note much. I should try my best to get rid of this habit.
