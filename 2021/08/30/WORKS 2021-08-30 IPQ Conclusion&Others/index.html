IPQ - Conclusion & Others

Posted on 2021-08-30

\Conclusion**

To sum up, this report analysed the current situation of United Airlines and provided suggestions for future development by using SPACE Matrix Analysis. We found that the whole industry suffered from the pandemic of COVID-19, although it is recovering slowly. United Airlines, the third-largest airline company in the world, is in challenging condition. In 2020, its profitability was negative, shown by the income statement. The cash flow statement was not optimistic, as it shows that United Airlines’ net income and net cash provided by operating activities decreased. Luckily, the balance sheet is improved because some indicators considered by the investors, like the total assets and stockholders’ equity, are all in advantaged situations. The future development is analysed using SPACE Matrix Analysis. We found that United Airlines may take defensive actions to survive during the pandemic of COVID-19.

There are several limitations in the present study. The main limitation is a lack of data while using the SPACE Matrix. For example, price elasticity of demand, the indicator of environmental stability, will be more accurate with the help of a questionnaire. However, because this is hard to carry out, we can only estimate it. Another limitation is that all the points are given by us subjectively. We do not have a scale to refer to.
