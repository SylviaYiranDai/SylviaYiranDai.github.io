Posted on 2021-07-18

\My next steps in planning, researching and deadlines that I will set myself. What I intend to do, by when, what resources I will use and how I will implement the recommendations of the centre coordinator (where appropriate).**

I have thought about the comments made by my supervisor about my proposal. She told me that it was better for me to narrow my topic down by employing fewer analysis methods because three methods may be redundant. I did some research on the three methods I employed, and I agree with her. As a result, I changed my title to “Analyse the influence of COVID-19 on the aviation industry and provide suggestions on their future development using SWOT & SPACE matrix analysis——Using United Airline as an example”. The change is because I found that both SPACE Matrix Analysis and PEST Analysis are about external analysis. A more narrowed title will allow me to pay more attention to one analysis method much more intensely, which will help me work out a more detailed report.

I also decided to organise my logic again. The discussion part will respond from 3 questions to 4 questions, including “How does COVID-19 influence the aviation industry?”; “How does COVID-19 influence Unit Airlines when looking at the three statements and stock condition?”; “What strategy will United Airlines employ in the future?”; “What detailed plan can United Airlines employ in the future?”. These questions help me build a connection between SPACE Matrix and SWOT Analysis. SPACE Matrix can act as the base of SWOT Analysis, as it can work out the strategy, and SWOT can be used to analyse the detailed plans within the strategy. From my point of view, this will make my report clearer and more logical.

Meanwhile, my supervisors and centre coordinator suggested that I develop more literature reviews, even including essays analysing other industries to work out the aspects of analysing. I started to read more essays. The resources, for example, include “The aviation industry: Tackling the turbulence caused by COVID-19” by Deborah Tay; “COVID-19 and business renewal: Lessons and insights from the global airline industry” by Joseph Amankwah-Amoah; “Will the Aviation industry have a bright future after the COVID-19 outbreak? Evidence from Chinese Airport shipping sector” by Jingxuan Liu.

\The reasons for my decisions.**

I plan to achieve this by reading essays I collected before. I also want to talk to a worker responsible for his or her company’s financial condition analysis and future development plan. He or she may help me analyse all the statements more clearly because of the practical experience.

I also want to learn more about the SPACE Matrix as it is a method I found instead of being taught in class. I may look at specific books which aim at explaining the principle and usage of SPACE Matrix.

\My summary of the comments and advice from my supervisor (and specialist consultant if applicable).**

During my meeting with my supervisor, I mainly received the positive feedbacks given by my supervisor. She said that my title and the main structure of my report were great because they are specific and focused. Also, my introduction, which includes enough background information and discussion about why it is a gap worth filling, is suitable. She also said that my methods are too complicated. My supervisor highlights the literature table and literature review, which means I need to write them more detailedly and carefully. Additionally, she thought only analysing United Airlines is not proper for me to use China as an example.

When discussing the structure of the essay, we decided to increase the words spent on the literature review and change words on the methodology because of the change in methods. Also, as I am doing a discussion-based essay, most information is collected from others essays and the companies’ annual or quarterly reports about their condition, especially the three statements.

When looking at my planning table, she thought it was detailed and suitable, which means it is achievable and reasonable. It would help me manage my time more appropriately in the future.

\Modifications I have made as a result of my discussion with my supervisor (and specialist consultant if applicable).**

In order to respond to my supervisor’s advice, I decided only to use SPACE Matrix analysis which I think is the most suitable one. This decision can help me work out the future strategy of United Airlines based on the background of COVID-19 more scrupulously.

Also, I have mentioned above, the questions I would pay attention to have been changed. Due to the change in methods, I need to change it again. I change them into six questions, including “How does COVID-19 influence the aviation industry (using ICAO’s annual report about the whole civil aviation industry?”; “How does COVID-19 influence Unit Airlines when looking at the three statements and stock condition?”; “What is the financial strength of United Airlines?”; “What is the industrial attractiveness of United Airlines?”; “What is the environmental stability of aviation industry?”; “What is the competitive advantage of the aviation industry?”.

I found a detailed article called “The SPACE Matrix: A Tool for Calibrating Competition” by Laetitia Radder and Lynette Louw. They explained the indicators in SPACE Matrix clearly and used three organisations in the South African Industry to explain.
